1SociaalDomein

1SociaalDomein is a community platform for people that work in healthcare. During my internship with Yard Digital Agency I got the job to rebrand 1SociaalDomein and redesign their website. I iterated on the original logo to create a new logo consisting of three shapes that represent a minimalist "1".

The shapes from the logo also come back in the design as background shapes and image containers so I really went all in on the consistency. As for the colors I used the existing green, made this color accessable for the WCAG AA guidelines and used the color wheel to find a good contrast color.
